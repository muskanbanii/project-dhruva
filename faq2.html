FAQ's
  • Are the demo classes free?

    Yes, they are free. More details about the demo classes will be provided by our counselor.

  • Do all programs have live-doubt solving?

    Live doubt-solving feature is available as a part of BAOIAM'S Classes offering.

  • Are BAOIAM'S programs mapped to the school syllabus/curriculum?

    Yes, the programs are personalised for students as per the school syllabus.

  • Where can I track my child's progress?

    A child's progress can be accessed through the Parent Zone section in the app. A progress report is available for every child and can be accessed by switching the profile.

  • What is a free demo class and how do I register for it?

    Your child can attend a free demo class by our top teachers to experience our programmes first-hand. This is a great way to understand BAOIAM'S way of teaching. Our counselor will help book a demo class for you at your convenience.
